About our Robotics & GPT AI Tech Challenge

The future is now here. The ever increasing rate of advances in technology has been pointing to this moment for some time. Recent breakthroughs in Artificial Intelligence brings us to this defining moment.

Our Robotics & GPT AI Tech Challenge is a groundbreaking initiative designed to harness the power of AI and robotics to transform the way we interact with technology. This challenge not only aims to foster innovation among young minds but also to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

By encouraging students to engage with cutting-edge AI and robotics technologies, we are paving the way for a new era of technological advancement that promises to revolutionize industries, improve quality of life, and create sustainable solutions for the challenges of tomorrow

What's in for you?

What can you expect from the Afrelib Tech Challenge?

Innovation at its finest: The Robotics & GPT AI Tech Challenge is the perfect opportunity to showcase creativity and technical expertise while solving real-world problems.

Cutting-Edge Technology: From robotics to Artificial Intelligence, this challenge is all about pushing the boundaries of what's possible using the latest technology.

Real-World Impact: The projects developed during this challenge have the potential to make a real difference in the world, providing solutions to some of the biggest challenges we face.

Collaboration: Participants have the chance to collaborate with like-minded individuals and learn from experts in the field, making this an invaluable networking opportunity.

Hands On Learning | Expert Instructors | Mentorship Our program emphasizes hands-on learning, providing students with ample opportunities to work on real-world projects, collaborate with peers, and receive feedback from experienced instructors. Our expert instructors, who bring real-world experience to the classroom, are passionate about teaching and dedicated to helping students succeed. They also offer mentorship and guidance throughout the challenge, helping participants build skills, gain knowledge, and navigate their learning journey effectively.

Our Recent Activities

Afrelib AI Tech Challenge 2023

The Journey The 2023 Afrelib Tech Challenge brought together students nationwide to tackle pressing environmental issues through innovation. The competition spanned several months, with participants advancing through rigorous stages, from concept development to project execution, showcasing their creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Achievements The challenge received over 500 submissions, with the top 10 projects presented at the grand finale. Participants demonstrated exceptional ingenuity in addressing global challenges such as climate action, food security, and water scarcity, fostering a deeper commitment to sustainability and innovation among the youth.

The Winning Project The winning project, an IoT Farm Irrigation and Monitoring Robot by Premier Team II, revolutionized smart agriculture by optimizing water use and monitoring crop health. This innovative solution, aligning with global sustainability goals, highlights the transformative potential of technology in creating a greener future.
