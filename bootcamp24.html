How our Summer Tech Bootcamp Works

Our summer camp runs for the span of 3 whole weeks from the 5th of August to the 24th August, 2024 with. Installation and Onboarding will be done on the first day of the bootcamp and the students will swing directly into their learning and projects from the second day of the Bootcamp.

Classes run from 9am - 1pm from Monday through to Friday Class. commences with the students participating in Aerobics and Exercises before the students progress to commence classes.
