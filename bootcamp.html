Tech Bootcamp

Empower Your Kids Future with Afrelib Academy Tech Bootcamps

Ignite Your Child's Passion for Technology and Innovation

At Afrelib Tech Bootcamp, we offer transformative technology experiences for children aged 6-18. Held twice a year in April and during the summer, our bootcamps are designed to equip young minds with essential tech skills and foster a love for innovation. Our programs include hands-on learning in coding, robotics, web development, content creation, and more. Through interactive sessions and expert guidance, your child will gain invaluable skills that pave the way for a successful future in the digital age.
